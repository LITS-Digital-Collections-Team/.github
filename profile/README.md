# Digital Collections Team at Hamilton College LITS

The Digital Collections Team is the primary steward of Hamilton College's digital archives and special collections.

Our work is never merely about delivering content — it is about building the human capacity to engage with historical documentation, scholarly discourse, and digital cultural heritage freely, critically, and independently within the context of a global information economy. We aspire to provide systems, collections, staff experts, and institutional support networks that serve a greater purpose than simple document storage and digital content provision. We aim to be a workshop and laboratory that empowers our community with the means of information, by developing tools and resources that grant researchers, students, and community members genuine command over the way knowledge is created, disseminated, accessed, and reused in everyday scholarly and civic life.
